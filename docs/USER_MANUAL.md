## 1. Getting Started

### Account Creation & Login
1.  **Register**: Navigate to the **Register** button in the top navigation bar.
2.  **Select Role**: Choose your primary role from the dropdown:
    *   **Project Owner**: For house/building owners and developers.
    *   **Contractor**: For licensed builders or technical teams.
    *   **Vendor**: For manufacturers and suppliers of materials.
    *   **Investor**: For institutional or private capital providers.
    *   **Government**: For public procurement officers.
3.  **Sign In**: After registration, use the **Login** page to access your specific dashboard using your email and password.

### Dashboard
Once logged in, your dashboard is your command center.
*   **Owners**: See active projects, contract status, and financial summaries.
*   **Contractors**: See open bids, active jobs, and pending payments.
*   **Investors**: See portfolio performance and compliance status.

## 2. For Project Owners & Developers

### Creating a Project
1.  Navigate to **Projects** > **List New Project**.
2.  Enter details: Title, Location, Budget, and Description.
3.  **Requirements**: Once created, go to the Project Detail page to add requirements (e.g., "500 Bags of Cement", "Electrical Wiring Team").

### Buying Materials
1.  Go to the **Marketplace** (`/`).
2.  Browse or search for materials.
3.  Add items to your cart and checkout.
4.  Funds are held in **Escrow** until delivery is verified.

### Hiring Contractors
1.  Post a **Contract Opportunity** (`/contracts/new`).
2.  Define the scope, budget range, and location.
3.  Review incoming **Bids** from verified contractors.
4.  **Award**: Select a winner to generate a digital contract.

### Managing Execution
*   **Milestones**: Break down the project into payable steps (e.g., "Foundation Laid").
*   **Approvals**: When a contractor marks a milestone as done, you receive a notification. Review the work.
*   **Release Funds**: Click "Approve" to release escrow funds to the contractor.

---

## 3. For Contractors & Suppliers

### Finding Work
1.  Go to **Contracts** (`/contracts`).
2.  Filter by your trade (Masonry, Electrical, Plumbing).
3.  **Submit Bid**: Propose your price and timeline.

### Getting Paid
1.  **Start Work**: Once hired, the contract moves to "Execution".
2.  **Update Progress**: Mark milestones as "Completed" in your dashboard.
3.  **Receive Funds**: Once the owner approves, funds are instantly transferred to your wallet (or bank account via Settlement).

### Listing Products (Vendors)
1.  Navigate to **Vendor Portal** -> **My Products**.
2.  Upload product photos, specs, and tiered pricing.
3.  Manage inventory levels real-time.

## 4. For Investors

### Compliance Onboarding (KYC)
*Regulatory requirement: You cannot invest until verified.*
1.  Go to **Investor Dashboard** (`/investor/dashboard`).
2.  Upload ID documents (Passport/National ID).
3.  Wait for "Verified" status (usually <24 hours).

### Funding Projects
1.  Browse **Projects**. Look for "Funding Open".
2.  Review the prospectus, risk score, and projected returns.
3.  **Pledge**: Enter your investment amount.
4.  **Sign**: Execute the digital **Investment Agreement**.
5.  **Fund**: Transfer capital to the project's segregated escrow account.

### Secondary Market
*Need liquidity?*
1.  Go to **Secondary Market** (`/market/secondary`).
2.  List your project stake for sale.
3.  Once another investor buys your stake, funds are settled to your account.

## 5. For Government & Compliance

### Public Tenders
1.  Navigate to **Public Tenders** (`/tenders`).
2.  **Publish Tender**: Define strict deadlines and evaluation criteria.
3.  **Audit Logs**: The system records every bid submission and view immutably.
4.  **Award**: transparently select the winner based on the recorded criteria.

### Regulatory Reports
*For Auditors/Regulators:*
1.  Go to **Reports Portal** (`/admin/reports`).
2.  Download **AML Logs**, **Tax Filings**, or **System Audits**.
3.  Data is exportable in JSON/CSV formats compatible with regulatory systems.

## 6. Core Platform Features

### Escrow & Trust
*   **Never Pay Upfront**: We hold buyer funds securely until obligations are met.
*   **Never Work for Free**: Contractors know funds are verified/locked before starting work.

### Dispute Resolution
*   If things go wrong, raise a **Dispute**.
*   Funds are **Frozen** automatically.
*   Admins review evidence (photos, chat logs) and arbitrate a fair Refund or Release.

### AI Risk Engine
*   **Fraud Detection**: The system analyzes patterns to block suspicious transactions.
*   **Reliability Scores**: Contractors and Owners earn scores based on past performance (on-time payments, quality work). Check scores before doing business!

### Modern Interface & Accessibility
*   **Centralized Styling**: The platform features a consistent, professional design built for efficiency and speed.
*   **Accessibility (WCAG)**: We prioritize high-contrast text and keyboard-friendly navigation to ensure the platform is accessible to all users across all devices.
