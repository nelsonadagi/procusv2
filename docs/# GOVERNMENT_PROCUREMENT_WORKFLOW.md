This document defines the workflow required to support public tenders and infrastructure procurement.

---

## 1. Objective

Enable governments and public institutions to:

* Publish tenders transparently
* Evaluate bids fairly
* Maintain anti-corruption audit trails

---

## 2. Tender Lifecycle

States:

* PUBLISHED
* OPEN_FOR_BIDS
* EVALUATION
* AWARDED
* EXECUTION
* COMPLETED

---

## 3. Compliance Requirements

Public procurement requires:

* Bid document submission
* Qualification verification
* Public award transparency
* Immutable audit logs

---

## 4. Anti-Corruption Controls

* All actions logged
* No bid modification after deadline
* Admin oversight + escalation

---

## 5. Reporting

Government dashboards require:

* Tender participation metrics
* Award fairness reporting
* Execution milestone visibility
