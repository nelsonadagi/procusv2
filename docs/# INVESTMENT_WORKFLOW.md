# INVESTMENT_WORKFLOW.md

## Phase 4 — Investment Commitment Workflow (Foundation)

This document defines the safe Phase 4 foundation for investor participation.

Phase 4 does NOT implement a regulated securities marketplace. It establishes commitment tracking.

---

## 1. Objective

Enable projects to attract capital through:

* Investor commitments
* Transparent milestone reporting
* Future upgrade path to regulated funding

---

## 2. Commitment Lifecycle

States:

* PLEDGED (intent)
* CONFIRMED (funds received)
* CANCELLED

---

## 3. Workflow

### Step 1 — Investor Browses Projects

Investor views project listings.

---

### Step 2 — Investor Pledges Commitment

Investor commits an amount.

No fund transfer required at pledge stage.

---

### Step 3 — Owner Reviews Commitments

Project owner can:

* Accept commitments
* Engage investors

---

### Step 4 — Phase 5 Upgrade

Phase 5 introduces:

* Regulated investment flows
* Escrow-backed deployment
* Investor reporting dashboards

---

## 4. Risk Controls

* Commitments are non-binding initially
* No public trading
* Admin oversight of suspicious activity

---

**Investment commitment workflow is now defined for Phase 4.**
