# BANKING_ESCROW_INTEGRATIONS.md

## Phase 6 — Licensed Banking & Escrow Integrations

This document defines the production-grade integration requirements for regulated financial operations.

---

## 1. Objective

Move from escrow foundation (Phase 3–5) into:

* Licensed escrow institutions
* Banking settlement rails
* Segregated client fund accounts

---

## 2. Required Integrations

### Escrow Partner Integration

* Escrow account creation via API
* Fund holding confirmation
* Release and refund execution

---

### Banking Settlement APIs

* ACH/SEPA/Wire settlement
* Local payment rails (Mpesa, etc.)
* Multi-currency clearing

---

## 3. Segregated Accounts

Platform must support:

* Client fund segregation
* Ledger reconciliation
* Audit-ready reporting

---

## 4. Compliance Controls

* Transaction monitoring
* Threshold-based alerts
* Regulatory audit trails

---

## 5. Phase 6 Deliverable

Escrow and payments operate through licensed financial partners.

---
