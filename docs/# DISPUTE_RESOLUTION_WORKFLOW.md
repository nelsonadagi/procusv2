# DISPUTE_RESOLUTION_WORKFLOW.md

## Phase 3 — Dispute Resolution & Arbitration Workflow

This document defines the structured dispute workflow required for escrow-backed construction commerce.

---

## 1. Objective

Provide a trusted mechanism to resolve:

* Non-delivery of materials
* Poor quality fulfillment
* Contractor milestone disagreements
* Payment release conflicts

---

## 2. Dispute Lifecycle

States:

* OPENED
* UNDER_REVIEW
* EVIDENCE_SUBMITTED
* RESOLVED_RELEASE
* RESOLVED_REFUND
* CLOSED

---

## 3. Dispute Initiation

A dispute can be opened by:

* Buyer
* Vendor
* Contractor

Triggers:

* Delivery failure
* Milestone rejection
* Fraud suspicion

---

## 4. Evidence Submission

Parties can submit:

* Photos
* Delivery receipts
* Messages/logs
* Contract documents

Stored securely with timestamps.

---

## 5. Escrow Hold

When dispute opens:

* Escrow funds are frozen
* Release is paused

Admin can apply `escrow_hold`.

---

## 6. Resolution Outcomes

### Release Funds

If contractor/vendor fulfills correctly.

### Refund Buyer

If fulfillment fails.

### Partial Split

For negotiated outcomes.

---

## 7. Admin Arbitration Controls

Admins must be able to:

* Review evidence
* Override milestone approval
* Trigger refunds/releases
* Ban fraudulent actors

---

## 8. Phase 3 Success Metrics

* Dispute resolution time < 7 days
* Dispute rate reduction ≥ 40%
* High trust adoption of escrow
