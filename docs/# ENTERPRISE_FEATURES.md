# ENTERPRISE_FEATURES.md

## Phase 5 — Enterprise Construction Suite Features

This document defines the enterprise-grade capabilities introduced in Phase 5.

---

## 1. Objective

Support large construction firms and institutional buyers with:

* Multi-organization procurement
* Approval workflows
* Supplier governance
* ERP integration readiness

---

## 2. Multi-Organization Accounts

Enterprises require:

* Organization entity
* Multiple users per org
* Role hierarchies (Requester/Approver/Admin)

---

## 3. Procurement Approval Workflows

Purchases and contracts require:

* Internal approvals
* Budget thresholds
* Audit logs

Workflow:

Request → Approve → Execute → Report

---

## 4. SLA Supplier Performance

Track:

* Delivery timeliness
* Fulfillment success
* Dispute frequency

Suppliers receive performance scores.

---

## 5. ERP Integration Readiness

Phase 5 prepares connectors for:

* SAP
* Oracle
* QuickBooks

Integrations occur in Phase 6.

---

## 6. Enterprise Success Metrics

* ≥ 10 enterprise accounts onboarded
* Procurement volume growth ≥ 50%

---
