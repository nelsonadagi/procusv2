## 1. Registering on the Platform

1. Navigate to the **Register** page.
2. Enter your email, first name, last name, and a secure password.
3. **Select your role carefully**: Your dashboard and available actions will be determined by this selection.
4. Click **Create Identity**.
5. Once registered, log in to access your role-specific console.

---

## 2. Role: Buyer (Procuring Materials)

As a Buyer, you use the platform to source high-quality construction materials from verified vendors.

### üõí Browsing and Ordering
1. **Explore Catalogue**: Use the "Materials" section to browse products. Use filters to find exactly what you need (e.g., Cement, Steel, Timber).
2. **View Details**: Click on a product to see description, base price, and availability.
3. **Add to Order**: Enter the quantity and click **Order Now**.
4. **Checkout**: Review your order summary and confirm the delivery address.

### üì¶ Tracking and Disputes
1. **Order Dashboard**: Monitor your orders in "My Orders".
2. **Status Tracking**: See when your order moves from `PLACED` to `SHIPPED` to `DELIVERED`.
3. **File Dispute**: If there is an issue with the quality or delivery, use the **Open Dispute** button to freeze the payment until resolved by an admin.

## 3. Role: Vendor (Selling Materials)

As a Vendor, you provide the "Liquidity" of materials on the platform.

### üèóÔ∏è Product Management
1. **List Products**: Use your dashboard to add new materials. Ensure you set accurate categories and stock levels.
2. **Inventory Updates**: Keep your stock levels updated. The system will automatically mark items as `OUT_OF_STOCK` if they reach zero.

### üöö Order Fulfillment
1. **Incoming Orders**: View new orders in your "Vendor Management" console.
2. **Status Updates**: As you process the order, move the status through `CONFIRMED`, `PACKING`, and `SHIPPED`.
3. **Fulfillment Rate**: Your reputation as a vendor is tracked by your fulfillment rate and delivery timeliness.

## 4. Role: Contractor (Providing Services)

Contractors are verified service providers who bid on construction tenders.

### üõ°Ô∏è Getting Verified
1. **Onboarding**: Fill out the Contractor Registration form.
2. **Certifications**: Upload your licenses and certifications.
3. **Admin Review**: Wait for a platform admin to verify your credentials. You cannot bid on contracts until verified.

### üî® Bidding and Execution
1. **Find Tenders**: Browse the "Contracts" marketplace for posted tenders.
2. **Submit Bid**: Enter your proposed cost and timeline.
3. **Winning Jobs**: If a Project Owner awards you the contract, it will appear in your "Active Jobs".
4. **Milestones**: Complete work according to the defined milestones. Once an owner approves a milestone, funds are released to you.

---

## 5. Role: Project Owner (Managing Construction)

Project Owners are the primary drivers of demand. They create projects, hire contractors, and procure materials.

### üèóÔ∏è Creating a Project
1. **Set Up Project**: Define your project (name, location, budget).
2. **Requirements (BoQ)**: Add the materials and services you need (e.g., "500 Bags of Cement", "Plumbing Services").
3. **Post Tenders**: Create a tender for the services required.

### ü§ù Hiring and Paying
1. **Review Bids**: Compare bids from different contractors. Look at their reliability scores and past reviews.
2. **Award Contract**: Select the best bid to start the work.
3. **Escrow Funding**: Deposit project funds into the secure Escrow account.
4. **Approve Work**: As the contractor completes milestones, review the work and click **Approve**. This automatically triggers the payment release from escrow.

---

## 6. Role: Investor (Funding Projects)

Investors provide capital to projects in exchange for stakes or returns.

### üìà Investing
1. **KYC Verification**: You must complete the identity verification process (Upload ID/Passport) to become a verified investor.
2. **Project Discovery**: Browse "Investment Opportunities" to find high-potential construction projects.
3. **Pledge Capital**: Submit a pledge amount.
4. **Sign Agreement**: Review and sign the legally binding investment agreement for the project.

### üîÑ Secondary Market
1. **Manage Stakes**: View your investment portfolio.
2. **Trade**: Use the "Secondary Market" to sell your stakes to other investors or buy into existing projects for immediate liquidity.

## 7. Role: Admin (Platform Management)

Admins oversee the health and regulatory compliance of the entire ecosystem.

### üî¨ Operational Oversight
1. **Audit Logs**: Monitor the `AUDIT_LOG_STREAM` to see every action taken on the platform in real-time.
2. **Verifications**: Review and approve new contractors and investor KYC documents.
3. **Dispute Resolution**: Mediate between buyers and vendors/contractors. Decide whether to **Refund** or **Release** held escrow funds.

### ‚öôÔ∏è System Configuration
1. **Platform Identity**: Update branding, contact info, and default regions.
2. **Currencies**: Manage exchange rates and active currencies (KES, USD, etc.).
3. **Taxonomy**: Manage the categories for products and services (Material, Service, Project types).
